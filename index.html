Zigbee Firmware Flashing Tool

Welcome to the Zigbee Firmware Flashing Tool. This tool allows you to easily flash firmware onto your Zigbee devices.

Features:

  • Flash coordinator or router firmware effortlessly.
  • Intuitive user interface for easy navigation.
  • Support for various Zigbee device models.
  • Real-time status updates during the flashing process.
  • Secure and reliable flashing procedures.

Instructions:

To use the Zigbee Firmware Flashing Tool, follow these steps:

  1. Connect your Zigbee dongle to the computer.
  2. Select the appropriate device model from the tool.
  3. Choose the firmware version you wish to flash.
  4. Click on the 'Flash' button and follow the prompts.
